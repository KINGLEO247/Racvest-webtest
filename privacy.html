Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Racvest collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you use our website, mobile app, and related services.

Effective date: April 14, 2026 Data use and protection overview

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, such as your name, contact details, account credentials, payment details, support messages, delivery information, and identity verification data where needed.

2. Operational and Device Data

We may also collect technical and usage information such as device type, browser details, IP address, app activity, approximate location, and interaction data that helps us operate, secure, and improve the service.

3. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage accounts.
  • To process deliveries, payments, verification, and support requests.
  • To improve platform safety, fraud prevention, and product performance.
  • To communicate service updates, transactional notices, and relevant support information.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share limited information with couriers, senders, payment providers, identity verification providers, analytics tools, customer support systems, and authorities where required by law or necessary to protect users and the platform.

5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and maintain secure business records.

6. Security

Racvest uses reasonable administrative, technical, and operational safeguards to protect personal information. However, no platform or transmission method can be guaranteed completely secure.

7. Your Choices

You may request account updates, corrections, or support regarding your personal data. Depending on your location and applicable law, you may also have rights related to access, deletion, objection, or restriction of processing.

8. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies, local storage, and analytics tools to remember preferences, understand usage patterns, measure performance, and improve the user experience across our services.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how Racvest handles personal data, contact us through the Contact page or at support@racvest.com.
